Who are we
Ajay and Prerna are health and fitness entrepreneurs based out of Jaipur, India. For more than half a decade now, they have been helping people transform themselves physically & mentally; assisting them in becoming a better version of themselves. The number of people directly impacted from their coaching and support is in the though-sands, but they have also inspired, and continue to inspire, millions through different social platforms and public speaking. As their influence grows they hope they can continue to be a source of positive inspiration for people worldwide
